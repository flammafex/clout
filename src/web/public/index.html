What is an Identity?

Your identity is your cryptographic account for signing posts and trust signals.

Think of it like your username + password combined into a keypair:

  • Public Key: Your visible address (like a username)
  • Identity Name: A local label for this keypair (only on your device)

Share your public key so others can trust you and see your posts.
